Xingbo Wu (吴兴博)


Senior Researcher at Microsoft Research Cambridge since January 2022.
My research interests lie in the area of performance and efficiency of computer systems.
I was a tenure-track assistant professor at UIC CS. I received my Ph.D. from UTA CSE, advised by Dr. Song Jiang.
